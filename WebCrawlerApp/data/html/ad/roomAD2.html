One-room apartment to sublet (45 sqm).

One month rental, from January 17th until mid-March (open end dates). Anmeldung is, unfortunately, not possible!

The living room area is equipped with a sofa bed (perfect for a person or a couple), two desks for home office, a full bath with tub, washing machine, dishwasher, a fully equipped kitchen, and a balcony. Third floor (with lift). Neubau building, is very bright, quiet, warm, peaceful and cozy.

The apartment is located in Mitte, Alexanderplatz. Alexanderplatz’s U-Bahn (u2, u5, u8) and S-Bahn train stations are just a short walk (5 minutes). There is a supermarket 100 meters away.

The cost (1500€ per month) is all-inclusive, with a deposit of 500€ (which will be reimbursed upon the end of the sublet)

Looking for someone or a couple (the apartment is not equipped for children nor pets) who is/are, professional, respectful, responsible, non-smokers and who will treat this place like their own home, especially our beloved plants!

The apartment is situated in a prime location in Mitte, Alexanderplatz, making it an ideal choice for your stay in Berlin. With its central position, you'll have easy access to all the attractions and amenities the city has to offer. Alexanderplatz's U-Bahn (metro) stations (u2, u5, u8) and S-Bahn (suburban train) station are just a short 5-minute walk away, providing convenient transportation links to explore the city and beyond.

In addition, you'll find a supermarket just 100 meters away, ensuring that your daily needs are easily met. The vibrant neighborhood offers a wide range of shops, cafes, restaurants, and entertainment options, allowing you to immerse yourself in the lively atmosphere of Berlin.

The apartment is nestled in a modern Neubau building, providing a tranquil and peaceful environment. Despite being centrally located, the area retains its calmness, offering you a comfortable retreat from the bustling city. Moreover, being on the third floor with a lift, you'll enjoy easy accessibility and pleasant views from the balcony.

Overall, this location combines the convenience of proximity to transportation and amenities with the tranquility of a peaceful neighborhood, making it an ideal base for your stay in Berlin.

- One-month rental: The apartment is available for sublet from August 17th until September 22nd.
- Anmeldung (official registration) is unfortunately not possible during this sublet period.
- The apartment is suitable for a single person or a couple. Please note that it is not equipped for children or pets.
- We kindly request non-smokers as tenants.
- The monthly rent of 1400€ is all-inclusive, covering utilities and Wi-Fi.
- A refundable deposit of 500€ is required, and it will be reimbursed upon the end of the sublet.
- We kindly ask for professional, respectful, and responsible tenants who will treat the apartment and our beloved plants with care.
- The apartment features a fully equipped kitchen, a full bath with a tub, a washing machine, and a dishwasher, providing you with all the necessary amenities.
- The apartment is located on the third floor and can be easily accessed via the lift.
- The building is a Neubau (new construction), offering a bright, warm, and cozy living space.
- We emphasize maintaining a peaceful and quiet atmosphere in the apartment.
