Sonstiges

The room has all that you need to live, work and study here. From appliances to linen and towels, internet and more.

🛏 Room description:
✔️ Double bed
✔️ Desk with chair
✔️ Armchair
✔️ Wardrobe
✔️ Mirror
✔️ Electrical appliances
✔️ Bedlinen and towels

The district boasts some of the city’s most renowned and thought provoking art galleries and cultural institutions, next to the best spots to go shopping or try some of the constantly opening new restaurants. If you ever want to catch a break: go check out Vabali Spa, the Balinese wellness oasis around the corner where you can swim under the stars and get one of the best massages in town.

About the apartment

The kitchen is fully stocked for any wannabe-chef to get creative in – you’ll have all the pots, pans and knives, as well as a fridge to keep things cool. The dining area is a great space to hang and invite your roomies over for dinner. All apartments entail one or two spacious bathrooms that you’ll be sharing with your room mates. To keep things clean and tidy, you can use the washing machine that’s placed in every apartment.

About the property

Located right around the corner from Berlin’s main train station this is your ideal home to explore all the hidden gems the city has to offer. If you want to stay in you can meet new friends watching documentaries at our cinema room, work a little while overlooking the city in our community spaces or take part in one of the yoga classes in the quite backyard. And if you fancy throwing a little party, you can do that in our hidden bar – sssssh, we’ll tell you where it’s at when you’ve moved in.
